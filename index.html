About Me

Edward McGee serves as a Pre-Mobilization Liaison (12T3O) with Alpha Team, 3-393rd Brigade Engineer Battalion. SSG McGee has been in the United States Military 19 Years, 8 Months and has served in the United States Navy for 6 years as an Aviation Warfare Operator assigned to a P-3 Squadron in Brunswick Maine, that included deployments to Puerto Rico and Iceland. 5 years active Mississippi National Guard. During his Active Army time, SSG McGee has been assigned to the 15th Engineer in Germany, and deployed to Kuwait while assigned to the 15th Engineers SSG McGee is SERE qualified Combat Life Savers, Combative Level One, Combative Level Two, Observer Coach/Trainer, Field Sanitation team certified, Advance Leader Course, Basic Leader Course, Hazmat Certified, Radiation Safety Officer . A native of Gulfport, Mississippi, SSG McGee has a 12-year-old daughter whom, he spends as much of his time allowed. SSG McGee is currently a student of UMUC, and is 48 hours away from his BS in Computer Network and Cyber security. SSG McGee has recently completed 590 hour, 9 month Network Engineering certificate taught by Cybertex Institute during his personal time from 1730 to 2130 Monday thru Friday. His personnel interests are sports, computers, playing the violin and painting.
